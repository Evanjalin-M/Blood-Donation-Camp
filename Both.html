Guidelines for Both

  1. General Safety Guidelines for Both Donors and Volunteers:
    • Follow Health Protocols: In case of ongoing public health concerns (e.g., COVID-19), ensure that all health protocols, like mask-wearing, temperature checks, and social distancing, are followed.
    • Sanitation: Use hand sanitizers frequently, and avoid touching your face.
    • Emergency Procedures: Be aware of emergency procedures in case a donor experiences complications. Ensure that trained medical staff are always available to handle such situations.
