ABOUT US

PURPOSE

To collect blood from voluntary donors to ensure a continuous and adequate supply for medical treatments and emergencies. Blood is a critical component of healthcare, used for surgeries, treating injuries, cancer therapies, childbirth complications, and many other medical conditions. Since blood cannot be artificially manufactured, blood donation is the only way to ensure availability.

OBJECTIVES

  • To Ensure Adequate Blood Supply
  • To Educate and Raise Awareness
  • To Recruit and Retain Voluntary Donors
  • To Foster a Spirit of Community Service
  • To Strengthen Public Health Systems
