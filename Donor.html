Guidelines for Blood Donors

  1. Eligibility Requirements:
    • Age: Donors should be between 18 and 65 years old.
    • Weight: A minimum weight of 50 kg (110 lbs) is required.
    • Health: Donors must be in good general health and free from any major illnesses.
    • Blood Donation Interval: At least 12 weeks (3 months) for males and 16 weeks (4 months) for females since the last donation.
    • Medications: Donors should not be taking medications that may affect the donation process (check with a doctor).
    • Recent Procedures: Donors should not have undergone any recent surgeries or dental procedures.
    • Travel: Those who have traveled to malaria-prone regions may be temporarily ineligible.
  2. Pre-Donation Instructions:
    • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water before donation to maintain good blood flow.
    • Eat a Healthy Meal: Have a balanced meal rich in iron (e.g., leafy greens, meat, or beans) 2-3 hours before donating.
    • Avoid Alcohol: Refrain from alcohol consumption for at least 24 hours prior to donating.
    • Rest: Ensure you get a good night's sleep before the donation day.
    • Documents: Bring valid identification, such as a government-issued ID or a blood donor card.
    • Dress Comfortably: Wear clothing with sleeves that can be easily rolled up.
  3. During the Donation Process:
    • Relax: Stay calm and follow the instructions of the medical staff.
    • Follow Health Checks: Be ready for basic checks like blood pressure, hemoglobin levels, and temperature.
    • Stay Still: Keep your arm still while the needle is in place, and report any discomfort or dizziness.
  4. Post-Donation Care:
    • Rest: Sit or lie down for a few minutes after donating.
    • Snacks and Hydration: Consume the refreshments provided, especially fluids and snacks, to replenish energy.
    • Avoid Strenuous Activities: Refrain from heavy exercise or lifting for the rest of the day.
    • Monitor for Reactions: Notify medical staff if you feel faint or unwell.
    • Hydrate: Continue drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
